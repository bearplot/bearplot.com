Features

Unified timeline

Every medication, meal, therapy session and more, all in a single chronological view. One glance tells you and your care team exactly where the day stands.

Incidents and observations

Log seizures, reactions, fever spikes, sleep, mood, pain and behavioural patterns. Spot trends and share what matters with your medical team.

Nutrition and metabolics

Log meals and saved meal plans alongside ketone and blood glucose readings. Share ready-made metabolic reports with your dietitian, purpose-built for metabolic diets.

Medication tracking

Log doses against prescriptions with flexible scheduling. Track what was given, when and by whom, with a full audit history.

Care team collaboration

Invite parents, grandparents, and nurses with role-based access. Every entry tracks who logged it and who performed the action.

Calendar view

See appointments, therapy sessions and observations on a single calendar. Review any day or week at a glance.
